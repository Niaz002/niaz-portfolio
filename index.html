01 — About

Designing with rhythm,
colour & a little noise.

Hi, I'm Niaz. I work as a Graphic Designer at Tahams, a creative company built on one idea: fill it, change it — nothing is ever finished, only remixed. That mindset shapes how I design: fast iterations, bold experiments, no fear of breaking a layout to find a better one.

I'm drawn to typography and poster making, bold pixel work, and colour that refuses to sit quietly. I also love bringing static frames to life through video — motion is just design that didn't want to stay still.
